About

One of these things I am learning is software development. I view Programming as a hobby I'd like to expand on. I enjoy making things myself and mainly focus around low-level development. But I'd like to think that I go wherever I kinda want to go.

Among programming, I have started messing around with Linux. Which has introduced me to the open-source community. I love the operating system and agree with the philosophies of open-source software.

Why "thepigeongenerator"?

I dislike having my full name and likeness out there. It is mostly a privacy concern. Therefore I needed a name, so I came up with the name. I love pigeons, I find them such interesting creatures. So intelligent too! When I was in need of a name, I did what my brain did best; mash different words together to make a coherent thought.
